Engineering Signal

How I think about systems

Bound the critical path

Keep user-facing paths explicit, observable, and latency-aware. Move non-critical work behind events when it improves resilience.

Design for provider failure

External providers are signals and dependencies, not guarantees. Degraded modes, retries, reconciliation, and audit trails need first-class design.

Make operations visible

Production systems need dashboards, logs, idempotency keys, state transitions, and manual recovery paths that support real incident handling.
